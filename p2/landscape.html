Move with the arrow keys, click to change terrain, enter a world seed for a new world.

Nick Warren - ncwarren

Artist Statement: My world represents a mountanous terrain, with rolling whispy clouds over the peaks. Additionally there are small lakes in the lowest points. I was inspired by timelapse videos of clouds, and the appearence of them always moving in one direction, yet staying over the peaks. Clicking the tile will change the color, so the user can draw new kinds of landscapes on the world.
